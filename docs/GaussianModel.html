Class: GaussianModel

GaussianModel(mean, covariance)

A multivariate Gaussian (normal) distribution model.

Represents: N(x | mean, covariance)

Provides:

  • likelihood(x): probability density at point x
  • sample(): random sample drawn from the distribution

The model supports arbitrary dimension d.

new GaussianModel(mean, covariance)

Parameters:

Mean vector μ of the Gaussian. Also exposed as .point.
